FAQ

Common questions

Can I present this as my agency's work?
Yes. Everything I deliver is yours to white-label. Your clients never need to know I exist. Most agencies mark up 50–100% and present the work as part of their own offering.
What if the project doesn't fit a package?
Packages cover the most common requests. If you need something different — a custom animation, a hybrid project, a different scope — just tell me on our intro call and I'll send a flat-rate quote within 24 hours.
What about timezone and communication?
I'm CET (Central European Time) — that's full overlap with UK/EU and US East Coast mornings. I work async via email or Slack, and I reply the same business day. No meetings unless you want them.
What if I need more revisions?
Each package includes 1–3 rounds depending on scope. If you need more, additional rounds are $75 each. In practice, most projects are done in the included rounds.
What formats and files do I get?
Every project includes the final exports in whatever formats you need (MP4, MOV, GIF, Lottie JSON, etc.) plus the source files (After Effects, Illustrator). You own everything.
How do I get started?
Book a 15-minute intro call. We'll talk about what your agency needs, I'll recommend a starting point, and if there's a fit, I can usually start the same week.
